# BLACKSAUCE - The Classic Collection

**Welcome to BLACKSAUCE!** 🎉

This is a website full of fun games and cool stuff to do. This guide will help you understand how everything works.

---

## What is BLACKSAUCE?

BLACKSAUCE is like a digital playground. It has:
- Games to play
- A chat bot to talk to
- Math quizzes
- Calculator
- And lots more!

The colors are red, black, and gold - that's what makes it look classic and special!

---

## How to Start

1. **Open the website**
   - Find `index.html` on your computer
   - Double-click it to open in your browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge)

2. **Sign In**
   - Type your name
   - Type your age
   - Click "Enter"

3. **Pick Something**
   - You'll see a menu with lots of choices
   - Click on anything that looks fun!

## Games You Can Play

### Tic-Tac-Toe
**How to play:**
- It's X vs O
- Take turns putting your symbol in squares
- Get 3 in a row to win!
- You can play against a friend or the computer

### Rock, Paper, Scissors
**How to play:**
- Choose rock, paper, or scissors
- Rock beats scissors
- Paper beats rock
- Scissors beats paper
- It's luck - try different choices!

### Number Guess
**How to play:**
- The computer picks a secret number (between 1-100)
- You guess what it is
- It tells you if you're too high or too low
- Try to get it in few tries as possible!

### Calculator
**How to play:**
- Click numbers
- Click +, -, ×, or ÷
- Click = to get the answer
- Perfect for homework!

### Math Quiz
**How to play:**
- It shows you a math problem (like 15 + 7)
- Type the answer
- If correct, you get a streak!
- See how many you can get right in a row

### Chat Bot
**How to play:**
- Type a message
- Press Enter or click send
- The bot will reply to you
- Ask it questions or just chat!

### Roast Game
**How to play:**
- Click the button to get roasted
- It's all in good fun!
- Share with friends

## Important Words

**Browser** - The program you use to view websites (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

**HTML** - The special code that makes websites work

**CSS** - The part that makes things look pretty (colors, shapes)

**JavaScript** - The brain that makes things move and work

## How to Edit Things (for parents or teachers)

If you want to change something:

1. **Change the name**
   - Open any .html file
   - Look for `<title>` at the top
   - Change the words inside

2. **Change colors**
   - Look for `:root {` in the CSS section
   - Change the hex codes (like #ff0000)
   - That's the color code!

3. **Add new games**
   - Copy one of the game files
   - Rename it
   - Change the game logic in the script

## Troubleshooting

**Page won't load?**
- Make sure you're opening the right file
- Try a different browser

**Game not working?**
- Refresh the page (press F5)
- Clear your browser cache

**Can't see the page?**
- Make sure your zoom is set to 100%
- Press Ctrl + 0 (or Command + 0 on Mac)
